The Retreat

Into the Mountain

This retreat is designed as an immersion into practice, landscape, culture, and stillness.

Each day unfolds slowly between yoga, meditation, study, shared meals, temple visits, mountain walks, and spacious time for rest. The intention is not simply to retreat from life, but to enter more deeply into it.

Set deep within Kerala’s high country, far from the tourist trail, the retreat offers the rare opportunity to experience India through intimacy rather than urgency — through rhythm, ritual, quietness, and attention.

Following the retreat, anyone wishing to continue travelling through India for an additional week is warmly welcome to join us.
